Transforming Lives Across Generations: Our Dedication to Corporate Social Responsibilities

At Indotronix, we are committed to building a better tomorrow through education. As advocates of equal opportunities, we strive to empower underprivileged children with access to primary school education, STEM learning, and personality development programs. By investing in the next generation of talent, we are creating a brighter future for our community and beyond.

  • Nigama Foundation: A school funded and operated under the leadership of CEO Sameer Penakalapati for children who are orphans or whose parents cannot afford to care for them.
  • Edison Career & Technology High School: Indotronix has been actively involved with a program dedicated to providing access to STEM education for inner-city youth who lack the means but have the aptitude.
  • Red Cross Youth Club: Indotronix is an enthusiastic sponsor of the Red Cross Youth Club since these clubs provide the tools and experience needed for attaining strong leadership, communication, and community service skills.
  • Youth programs: Indotronix is proud to provide ongoing funding of various youth programs for children in need through the YMCA and their affiliated programs.
